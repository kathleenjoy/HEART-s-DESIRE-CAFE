ABOUT

Goals/Aims

Product – We aim to serve the best quality and most interesting varieties of coffees, teas, cakes, frappes and pops at all times to everyone.

Service – We strive to treat our costumers respectfully as our guests; to offer our guest service that is prompt, efficient, friendly, relaxed and fair.

Atmosphere- We will provide a pleasant, welcoming environment, and maintain a clean and attractive café at all times.

Work Environment- We aim to create a positive, respectful workplace and to empower our staff to be themselves and to achieve their fullest potential.

Description

Heart’s Desire Café will offer its customers the best tasting coffee beverages in the area. This will be achieved by using high-quality ingredients and strictly following preparation guidelines. This café is committed to values such as excellence, passion, quality, integrity and leadership which allow them to navigate challenges and provide for future opportunities. These core beliefs start with their commitment to their products and their employees.

The Heart’s Desire Café rewards excellence and cherishes loyalty. The café will work with its employees to build strong businesses and a secure future.

Heart’s Desire Café will be a café located in the City of San Fernando, La Union near DMMMSU-MLUC. The café will serve coffees, teas, frappes, pops and cakes. The café owner is Mr. and Ms. Raña but their daughter Kathleen Joy Raña manages it. The café will be open for business Monday-Saturday, 4 am – 11 pm and closed on Sundays.

Heart’s Desire Café will provide a quiet and relaxing environment to enjoy coffees and sweets and an opportunity to visit with friends, catch up on one’s email or read a chapter of a book. The Café’s Baristas will be extremely knowledgeable and offer assistance in any coffee purchase while simultaneously providing customers with outstanding service.

OWNER'S PROFILE

Kathleen is so glad when her mother gave the permission to manage the cafe her mother and father built.
